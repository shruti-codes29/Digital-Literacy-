Ransomware Case Study – Synnovis Attack (Human Version):

In June 2024, a ransomware attack targeted Synnovis, a company that provides pathology services to hospitals in London. Ransomware is a type of cyberattack where hackers block access to systems and demand money to restore it.

In this incident, a hacker group called Qilin entered the company’s system, encrypted important files, and also stole sensitive patient data. This type of attack is known as double extortion because the attackers not only demand money to unlock the system but also threaten to leak the data if the payment is not made.

Due to this attack, many hospital services were affected. Hundreds of surgeries and appointments had to be cancelled because essential services like blood testing were disrupted. The attackers demanded a large ransom, but the company refused to pay, which led to the data being leaked.
