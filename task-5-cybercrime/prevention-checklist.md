# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
- Always verify unknown calls, messages, or links before responding
- Do not click on suspicious links or download unknown files
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
- Regularly update your apps and devices for security

## UPI / Financial Safety:
- Never approve unknown UPI payment requests (you will lose money)
- Do not scan random QR codes sent by strangers

## Reporting Cybercrime:
- Report online fraud at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
