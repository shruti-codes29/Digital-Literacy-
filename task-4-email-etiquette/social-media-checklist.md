# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## Do's:
- Use a professional profile name and photo
- Think before posting any content online
- Respect others and communicate politely
- Share useful and positive content
- Use strong passwords to keep accounts safe

## Don'ts:
- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not engage in online arguments
- Avoid accepting requests from unknown people
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation
